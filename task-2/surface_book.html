Search and Selection

As a strategic recruitment partner, Renaissance brings an Executive Search approach to all recruitment mandates. Our service offering focuses on middle level to Senior, Director and C-suite executives across the following specialisms:

  • Human Resources & Business Support
  • Information Technology
  • Legal
  • PR & Communications, Digital And Social Media
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Aviation
  • Banking & Finance
  • Business Consulting

Human Resources

A consultative approach to Human Resources where-by Renaissance obtains an understanding of your business and provides to Clients experienced HR professionals who becoming an extension to your Leadership Team on a Consultancy basis to support, deliver and meet the demands of your business.

Executive Coaching

Executive Coaching involves a series of one-on-one interactions between the Leader and an External Coach. A powerful leadership resource and tool that develops you, whether you are an Individual Leader, a part of a Senior Management Team or seeking to step-up into a high potential leading role.
